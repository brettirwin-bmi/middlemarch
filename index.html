About

Middlemarch is an executive search and leadership advisory firm. The name comes from George Eliot's novel about people striving to do consequential things in complicated circumstances.

Executive Search

The search for the right leader is not a commodity, it is a craft. It requires judgment that cannot be automated or rushed. We work quietly on a limited number of engagements.

Leadership Assessment

By assessing a leader from every direction, a 360 assessment identifies the gap between intention and impact, and gives organizations the information they need to act on it.

Executive Coaching

Leadership is not a fixed trait. Executive coaching helps leaders see themselves clearly, adapt deliberately, and perform exceptionally.
