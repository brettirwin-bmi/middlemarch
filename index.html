About

Middlemarch is an executive search and leadership advisory firm. The name comes from George Eliot's novel about people striving to do consequential things in complicated circumstances.

Executive Search

The search for the right leader is not a commodity, it is a craft. It requires judgment that cannot be automated or rushed. We work quietly on a limited number of engagements.

Leadership Assessment

By evaluating a leader from every angle in an organization, a 360 assessment identifies the gap between intention and impact, and gives the organization the information needed to close it.

Executive Coaching

Leadership is not a fixed trait. Coaching helps leaders see themselves clearly and adapt deliberately. We also train executives how to leverage AI as a strategic thinking partner, not just a productivity tool.
