About
Middlemarch is an executive search and leadership advisory firm. The name comes from George Eliot's novel about people striving to do consequential things in complicated circumstances.
Executive Search
The search for the right leader is not a commodity, it is a craft. It requires judgment that cannot be automated or rushed. We work quietly on a limited number of engagements.
Leadership Assessment
By assessing a leader from every direction, a 360 assessment identifies the gap between intention and impact, and gives organizations the information they need to close it.
Executive Coaching
Leadership is not a fixed trait. Coaching helps leaders see themselves clearly and adapt deliberately. We also train executives to leverage AI as a strategic thinking partner, not just a productivity tool.
