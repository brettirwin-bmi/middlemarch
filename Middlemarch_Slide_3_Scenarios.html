360 Leadership Assessment:
Four common scenarios
Revenue up, retention down.
Targets are being met but good people are leaving. A 360 looks for the explanation the exit interviews don't surface.
The coaching baseline.
A coaching engagement needs a starting point. A 360 provides one.
Can this person scale?
Managing a team and leading a business are not the same thing. A 360 tests whether the role is too large for the person in it.
The upstairs/downstairs split.
The leader is celebrated from above and resented from below. A 360 is commissioned to understand why.
